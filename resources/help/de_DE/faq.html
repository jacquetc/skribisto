FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

General

Where could I ask my question ?

If you don't find your answer in this FAQ or in the manual, you can always ask here.

Navigation

How could I quickly open an item on a new view ?

The quickest is to middle click on the item.

Text

How could I change the width of the main text area ?

On the right side, in the "Edit" tool box, click on the "A" icon with the up arrow. A new menu must appear.

Development

How could I help translate ?

Complete instructions are here.

To shorten : translate here (is's free) or create an issue here if your language is missing.

How could I contribute to the code ?

Firstly, before writing any change, maybe you can create an issue describing your modification, so we can discuss about it and so I can guide you ?

After that, you can fork the repository, make your change, then create a Push Request from your repository to mine.
